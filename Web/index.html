How to Rocket

REQUIREMENTS
If you have all of these, you're set!
Device iPhone or iPad
iOS / iPadOS 14.0 +

*Currently, How to Rocket only supports iOS 14.0 or greater, but dont worry, we're working hard to add iOS 13 support!

DESCRIPTION

Have you ever been in argument about the specific impulse of the Rocketdyne RS-25? Well look no further than How to Rocket. Just open the parts tab and search for the RS-25! Now you can always be right, with How to Rocket by your side! The app also has a learn tab, where you can learn all about the mechanics of rocketry, and prove once and for all that full flow staged combustion is best. Along with both those features, How to Rocket also includes a news feed with all the latest space news compiled into a single screen for your viewing pleasure. Please note How to Rocket is in VERY early alpha and currently the news feed is the only aspect of the app that is working properly
